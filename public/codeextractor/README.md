# CodeExtractor NodeJS Library

This is the **CodeExtractor [NodeJS](nodejs.org) Library** which is providing a collection of
helpful methods fot the **GICAT** Tool.

**GICAT** is developed by the _Computational Science Studies Lab_ of the _**Chair of Theory of Science and Technology**_ at _RWTH Aachen University_.


Further information for the use in your own projects will be provided soon.
